• You can setup a simple alias to any URL. google https://www.google.com/
  • Then all you need to do is type 'u ' in your browsers' address bar and your location will load your aliases URL.

    u google

  • Additionally you can add place holders into your url that can be replaced with additional parameters passed in your browsers' address bar. Placehoder Format: [_\d+]

    google https://www.google.com/?q=[_1]

  • URLs with place holders will replace those placeholders with additional parameters passed in your browsers' address bar.

    u google searchtext
